ACTION - 
A function that returns what we want to do. Describes what we want to do.

REDUCER -
Looks at the action. Based on what the action is, it modifies the state.
Place where you do the logic to what will happen to the state.

DISPATCH - 
Send action to the reducer. 

useSelector - 
extract anything from redux store. Gives us access to the full react store.

useDispatch - 
we use the dispatch every time we want to dispatch a specific action. 
on click of a button, useEffect etc dispatch a specific action.
